## 📌 Overview

A visually engaging tribute page dedicated to three extraordinary figures bound by the Oromo language, faith, and the pursuit of justice:

- **Onesimos Nesib** (c.1856–1931) — Translator who gave the Oromo people the Bible in their own tongue
- **Aster Ganno** (c.1872–1964) — Folklorist who preserved 500 traditional Oromo proverbs, riddles, and songs
- **Gudina Tumsa** (1929–1979) — Theologian and martyr who lived and died for holistic faith and social justice
